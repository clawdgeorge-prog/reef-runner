Design Priorities
  • Great graphics first: bloom-style glows, depth, particles, ambience, readable color hierarchy
  • Simplified game loop for stronger polish and cleaner feel
  • Mobile-friendly controls with tap-to-start, live drag steering, and forgiving lane turns
  • Not Pac-Man themed — more sleek sci-fi pursuit than cartoon maze munching
Current Gameplay
  • Collect prism shards to charge the breach meter and open the district exit
  • Reach the exit portal once it opens to complete the level and rotate districts
  • Discharge pulse to scatter nearby drones
  • Pulse through an active decoy to fire an arc lash across the lane
  • Use gates to break chase routes and reposition cleanly
  • Deploy a decoy beacon to bend pursuit lines and buy a clean escape lane
  • Each district now carries a live gameplay trait, not just a palette swap
  • Distinct enemy classes now force different route choices
  • Hunter drones now telegraph lock-ons before a high-speed surge
  • Skim drones to trigger Overdrive, converting close calls into temporary speed, charge, and score pressure
Controls: WASD / Arrow keys • Space = Pulse • Shift / X = Decoy • Drag anywhere on mobile to steer continuously with turn assist • Tap the arena to start or pulse.

Maze Sentinel

Slip through a luminous security lattice, charge your pulse core, and bend pursuit lines to your advantage. This build now has a cinematic intro, a district-reactive soundtrack that swells during hunter locks and exit rushes, telegraphed hunter surges, richer environmental FX, a decoy-to-pulse arc lash, a proper breach objective, and district traits that change the run rules each level.
